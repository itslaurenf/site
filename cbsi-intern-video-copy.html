CBSi Intern Video

CBS Interactive (2014)

Documentary-style marketing piece capturing the company culture and intern experience at CBS Interactive, made during my summer there in 2014 as an effort to support university outreach.

 

In September 2014 this video went live on the official CBS Interactive career pages (CBSi Internships, InternMatch/LookSharp...) as well as on the CNET Youtube page.

 

I directed, produced, filmed, edited... really carried this project through from start to finish, working closely alongside HR. I organized and conducted interviews, shot b-roll on a variety of cameras, and condensed ~3 hours of footage into 3.5 minutes over the course of three months.
